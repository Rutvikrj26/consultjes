About JES Engineering

Your trusted partner in global engineering procurement for over 15 years

Engineering Excellence Through Global Partnerships

JES Engineering is a leading procurement specialist connecting businesses with world-class engineering solutions. We partner with premier manufacturers across the globe to deliver quality-tested hydraulic systems, linear motion technology, and industrial cooling solutions.

With over 15 years of experience in the global supply chain, we understand the critical importance of reliability, performance, and precision in industrial applications. Every product we supply undergoes rigorous quality testing and meets international certification standards.

Our expertise spans three core product families: THM Huade's comprehensive hydraulic systems, QLH's precision linear motion technology, and AMI's advanced cooling solutions. This diverse portfolio enables us to serve industries from construction and manufacturing to agriculture and marine applications.
